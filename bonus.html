Green Neeson

Green Neeson wears green color dress. He will run very fast in greenary. He runs and saves the people in need.
He has a green color light ,by touching the rays of thatlight ,all the wounds of people will get disappeared. He will run faster than a aeroplane and reach to the places to help.

Directions
  1. Start from maryville to Kansas.
  2. Catch a flight to Washington.
  3. From there take a cab and start drive to Gifford Pinchot National Forest, Washington.
  4. There you can find Green Neeson in the entrance of Forest.
