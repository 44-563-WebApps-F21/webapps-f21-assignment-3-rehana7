Dazzling Woman

Dazzling Woman is having a Red and blue dress. She wares a brown head band with a star on it. She has a loose hair.

Dazzling Woman has the powers to fly up to a height of ten feet and saves any one met with accidents. She can lift the people on her hair, which forms like a carpet when she is flying.
